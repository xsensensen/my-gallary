About me

We are a New York-based agency uniting strategy and design to make work that evolves with the world we live in.

Our Approach

At PORTO ROCHA, we believe design should be both functional and beautiful. Our work is built on a foundation of strategic thinking, with a focus on creating meaningful connections between brands and people.

We are committed to crafting identities that are not only visually striking but also adaptable and forward-looking. Each project begins with deep research and ends with a thoroughly considered solution.

Through our collaborative process, we create work that resonates across cultures and contexts, helping our clients navigate an ever-changing world with clarity and purpose.

Our Team

Founded by Leo Porto and Felipe Rocha, our studio brings together a diverse team of designers, strategists, and creative thinkers. We value collaboration, experimentation, and a thoughtful approach to every challenge.

Our international perspective informs our work, allowing us to create designs that resonate across cultures and contexts. We believe that the best solutions come from diverse teams working together toward a shared vision.

Each member of our team brings unique skills and perspectives, creating a studio environment where innovation thrives and conventional thinking is constantly challenged.

Our Values

  • Clarity — We believe in the power of clear, straightforward communication.
  • Adaptability — Our solutions evolve with the changing needs of our clients and their audiences.
  • Collaboration — We work closely with our clients, treating them as partners in the creative process.
  • Innovation — We constantly challenge ourselves to explore new approaches and perspectives.
  • Craft — We pay meticulous attention to detail in everything we create.
